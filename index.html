What's in Your Belly Button?

Many bacterial species can be found within the human belly button. And each persons' collection of belly button bacteria is unique to them! Our goal is to find a bacteria that can synthesize proteins that taste similar to beef.

Could your belly button be the secret that unlocks this savroy success? Explore the dataset to find out more. Click on a test subject ID to find out more about their bacterial makeup, as well as how frequently they wash their navels.
